Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Jean-Auguse-Dominique Ingre The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.
